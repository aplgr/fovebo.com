Working with Fovebo

Remote B2B engineering services from Paraguay, structured for EU-based clients who value clear boundaries, predictable coordination and security-conscious access.

Fovebo provides remote B2B engineering services from Paraguay through Fovebo LLC, based in the United States. The approach is pragmatic: the collaboration framework should be clear before the first ticket is opened. The model is intentionally designed so EU companies can clarify responsibilities, compliance rules and security standards before the project starts.

This page is a practical overview of the collaboration model. It is not legal advice and does not replace your internal review.

A clear B2B service-provider framework

Collaboration is designed as a pure business-to-business relationship. Fovebo acts as an external partner, not as part of the client's internal organization.

  • No integration into internal duty rosters or fixed 9-to-5 presence requirements.
  • No authority to legally or commercially represent the client.
  • No acting on behalf of the client in interactions with the client's end customers unless expressly agreed.

Remote work with reliable coordination

Fovebo works remote-first and largely asynchronously. Instead of rigid presence requirements, the work is aligned with measurable outcomes: tickets, pull requests, deliverables and clearly defined acceptance criteria.

For coordination with engineering and product teams, fixed core availability windows or overlap windows can be agreed. This enables fast decisions when urgent questions arise while preserving the space needed for focused deep work.

Clear data and access boundaries

Access to client systems is strictly task-based. Fovebo follows the least-privilege principle and uses staging environments, anonymized datasets or test systems whenever possible.

  • Production access is used only when absolutely necessary and explicitly approved.
  • No local copies of client data are created on Fovebo systems.
  • Permission profiles and offboarding processes are agreed together before the project starts.

Security-conscious delivery

Fovebo works with disciplined handling of client systems. The infrastructure includes encrypted workstations, professional password management and multi-factor authentication (MFA/2FA). Access is only performed through client-approved routes such as VPN, SSO or SSH.

Infrastructure security

Fovebo also protects its remote work setup against short-term outages through redundant connectivity such as Starlink and battery-backed power supply through UPS systems.

German working style, global location

Fovebo is not an anonymous network. Behind it is a coordinated team with German roots. We bring many years of experience from demanding and security-sensitive environments into client projects and combine familiar reliability with the flexibility of modern remote work. More information about our backgrounds is available under "Our Team".

Contractual clarity

Fovebo's contracts reflect the practical collaboration model: confidentiality, intellectual property (IP), data protection and security expectations are clearly covered. Additional data processing agreements (DPA), standard contractual clauses (SCC) or security annexes can be provided where the client's process requires them.

The Fovebo General Terms are available online at any time.

Transparent boundaries

Some companies have strict compliance policies requiring service providers to be physically located within the EU. If that is an exclusion criterion, Fovebo is probably not the right fit. But if the question is whether remote B2B engineering can be structured securely and professionally across continents, Fovebo is ready for that conversation.
