To create a PayPal account, go to paypal.com and click on "Sign Up". Choose between a personal or business account, enter your details, and link a valid email address. Follow the instructions to verify your identity and you’re ready to send or receive payments.

After logging into your PayPal account, go to the "Wallet" section and select "Link a bank account". Enter your bank details and confirm the account by verifying the small deposits PayPal sends to your bank. This process usually takes 1-2 business days.
