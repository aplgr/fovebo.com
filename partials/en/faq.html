Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about collaboration, billing, and practicalities.

How does remote collaboration from Paraguay work for EU clients?

Fovebo works remotely from Paraguay through Fovebo LLC as a B2B contractor. For EU-based clients, the collaboration can be structured around clear contractor boundaries, agreed overlap windows, least-privilege access, security-conscious delivery and contract terms for confidentiality, IP/work results and data protection. Some companies require workers to be physically located in the EU; if that is a hard policy, this setup may not fit.

How does billing work?

Invoices are issued by Fovebo LLC (Florida, USA).
We can invoice in USD (default) or EUR upon agreement.
Standard payment terms are Net 14 – 30.

Security and confidentiality?

We work under NDA when required. Access follows your standards (VPN/SSO/SSH keys). We keep credentials out of code, rotate tokens, and avoid production data on local machines unless explicitly approved.

What does a typical start look like?

1) Short discovery call
2) Access & priorities
3) First delivery within the agreed scope.

We keep it lightweight and transparent from day one.

Do you only do modernization, or also maintenance?

Both. We stabilize what runs today (bug fixes, performance, DB care) and prepare the path forward (clean interfaces, stepwise migration to Go-based services).

Projects differ, so we quote individually. Share your context-scope, timeline, constraints - and we'll provide a clear proposal with scope, timelines, and commercials. - Transparent terms, no hidden fees.
